ABOUT ME

Hi. I’m Michelle, your architect turned UI/UX designer. Creating novel human experiences is a personal fulfillment of mine. I think like an architect and execute like a design creative. With my hands on experience in designing web and mobile interfaces, combined with the qualities I acquired from my formal training as a nit-picking architect, I am able to make the best of these two worlds and make it my own. I love to create web, app, and design products that are not only usable, but also pleasing and reflective of the company’s values.

Fun facts

Hobbies, Quirks, and Plain CRAZINESS.
Just a little peek into my life and the things that made me the person that I am today

Arithmetic Wiz

I used to be a mental math champ back in the days. Training for hours at a time, I won competitions across cities in Indonesia - even internationally. I must have over 50 trophies lined up in a cabinet at home. Unfortunately, these magical powers seem to have disappeared completely as I grew up and found the wonders of a calculator.

Harry Potter Fangirl

Ever since I could read "big people books" I have pored over the pages of every single Harry Potter novel! I can't recall the number of times I've read through the series - not to mention watching the movies! Even after the story ended, I would look for fan-based writings to keep the excitement going! If you happen to be a fan too, here's one of my favorites!

Self-Proclaimed Guitarist

I love anyone who plays the guitar! (plus points for singing too) Me? Well, I try. I'm a mediocre self-taught guitarist - but I love playing it! I'm learning, and some of my sweet friends bought me a guitar for my birthday. I love to make silly covers with my friends, and here's just one example of the many crazy things we do!

Figure Skater

I love playing with snow - but I grew up in Indonesia, a tropical country where the seasons are only rainy and.. well, rainy. So what's the next best thing? Figure skating! I can still vividly remember the rush of cold air passing through my cheeks as I raced around the skating ring with my friends instead of practicing my turns on ice...

Snowboarder Wannabe

This is a relatively new hobby I picked up a few years ago. Since I moved to the United States, I discovered the glory of Winter time! Skating, snowboarding, snow-mobiling - you name it! Coming from a tropical country, seeing snow gets me excited. Now I snowboard with my friends every year, and I don't see myself stopping anytime soon.

Little Ballerina

For a while, I was a twirler too. I started as a little ballerina - but eventually branched out and did other dance types. I particularly enjoyed my time in the performing arts club while I was in grade school. I'd like to think that I was a pretty good dancer then - but I guess all that flexibility and skill dissipated as I grew older. :(

Typical Asian Pianist

Talk about stereotypes! Yes, I play the piano. Yes, I started with classical piano. Yes, I spent hours and hours a day practicing. But no, my mum is not a tiger mum who beats me up or forces me to play the instrument. :) I have since moved on from classical piano and now play the keyboard in my church team!
